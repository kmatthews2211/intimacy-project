3-month Immersion & Retreat

Immersive Yoga Programme

Join us for our brand new 3-month online immersive yoga programme, with the option to take your practice to the next level at our 10-day retreat in Morocco.

Over 3 months, you will learn on an experiential level, through relationship and real life, the true meaning of yoga. You will be supported, each step of the way, with weekly group classes, discussions and individual 1:1 support.

You will have the opportunity to meet and share your experiences with others who are going through the same process, supporting each other along the way through challenges and changes. And at the end, we celebrate together, in person!

We are here to help you do your yoga.

How does it work?

Across a 3-month period, we will be offering online weekly group classes, discussions and 1:1 support, culminating in our optional 10-day Moroccan retreat.

Whether you are a complete beginner, or advanced in your yoga practice, we work together to help develop the best practice for you. As you grow, we endeavour to make you feel nurtured, nourished and supported to allow you to be more peaceful, harmonious and loving with yourself and others.

For 3 months online: £550/€630 (or 3 instalments of £170/€195)

For 3 months online + 10-day retreat in Morocco: £1750/€2008 (or 3 instalments of £590/€677)

Each week you will receive:

  • Guided group yoga class.
  • Group themed discussion.
  • One private 1:1 session .
  • Group Yoga Nidra session.
  • Optional morning practice space in our zoom room Monday-Friday.
  • And at the end, we celebrate together in Morocco for a 10-day retreat to delve deeper into all that you explored to solidify all that you have integrated within your daily life in the 3 months.
