40 Day Immersion

Create and develop a daily yoga practice into your life.

Join us online for our 40-day personalised yoga immersion programme, where we share our unconditional love, support and experience to help you develop an intimate daily yoga practice.

By linking breath and body movement, you will begin to feel fulfilment, contentment and positive change in every aspect of your life.

We are here to help you do your yoga.

How does it work?

We hold a daily online session together, across 40 days (Monday to Friday), at a time that is suitable for you and your schedule.

Whether you are a complete beginner and wanting to learn the basic yoga principles, advanced in your practice and wishing to integrate a more purposeful practice into your life, or wishing to take the next step in your yoga to teach, we work together towards a shared goal to help you develop the best practice for you.

As you grow, we endeavour to make you feel nurtured, nourished and supported to allow you to be more peaceful, harmonious and loving with yourself and others.

Cost: £750

What can I expect?

  • 1:1 support to guide you on your yoga journey, at a time that fits with you each day.
  • Support in your physical practice, designed to meet your needs.
  • Our sessions are held on Zoom and are approximately an hour each day, although this is flexible.
  • For those wishing to teach yoga, we offer a 100-hour yoga certificate certified by The Intimacy Project and accredited by Heart of Yoga. (You can choose to continue your journey with our 1:1 Mentorship and gain your 200-hour certificate).
